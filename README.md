            <h2>Vampire Overview</h2>
            <p>The vampire is one of horror's most famous undead creatures. In folklore and gothic fiction, vampires are often described as corpses that return from the grave to feed on the blood or life force of the living. They are linked to night, aristocratic castles, coffins, curses, seduction, and the fear of becoming something no longer human.</p>

          <p>Vampires often hunt at night and use charm, deception, or supernatural influence to approach victims. Some stories describe them as elegant and intelligent, while others show them as corpse-like predators driven only by hunger.</p>

          <h2>Signs of a Vampire</h2>
